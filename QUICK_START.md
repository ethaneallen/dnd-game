## 🎲 Step 5: Play!

### Basic Actions

**🔍 Explore** - Discover new areas and encounters  
**🗺️ Travel** - Move to different locations  
**🛏️ Rest** - Recover HP and spells  
**👥 Party** - Manage companions  
**📜 Journal** - Check quests  
**💾 Save** - Save your progress

### Combat Actions

**⚔️ Attack** (or press **A**) - Strike your enemy  
**🛡️ Defend** (or press **D**) - Increase your AC  
**✨ Spell** (or press **S**) - Cast magic (if applicable)  
**🏃 Retreat** (or press **R**) - Try to escape

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for New Players

### ✅ DO:
- **Save often** using the Save button
- **Rest** when your HP is low (you'll find rest areas)
- **Explore** thoroughly to find items and gold
- **Recruit companions** at taverns for help in combat
- **Use spells wisely** (they're limited until you rest)
- **Check your inventory** - use potions in tough fights

### ❌ DON'T:
- Skip reading the story - it's engaging!
- Fight when you're low on HP - rest first
- Ignore side quests - they give great rewards
- Forget to equip better items you find
- Waste spell slots on easy enemies

## 📊 Understanding Your Character Sheet

### Left Panel - Your Stats

**HP Bar (Red)** - Your health  
**XP Bar (Purple)** - Progress to next level

**Six Main Stats:**
- **STR** - Strength (melee attacks)
- **DEX** - Dexterity (ranged attacks, AC)
- **CON** - Constitution (HP, endurance)
- **INT** - Intelligence (wizard spells)
- **WIS** - Wisdom (cleric spells, perception)
- **CHA** - Charisma (social interactions)

**AC** - Armor Class (higher = harder to hit)  
**Gold** - Your money

## 🎯 First Quest Tips

### In Your First Combat:
1. Click **Attack** (or press **A**)
2. Watch the combat log - it shows everything
3. If HP gets low, use **Defend** to reduce damage
4. Keep attacking until you win!

### After Your First Combat:
- You'll gain XP and possibly gold
- Check inventory for any new items
- Equip better weapons if you found any
- Continue exploring!

### Getting Stronger:
- **Level up** by gaining XP from combat
- **Find better equipment** while exploring
- **Recruit companions** to help you fight
- **Complete quests** for bonus rewards

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Game won't start?
- Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Edge 90+, Safari 14+)
- Enable JavaScript in your browser
- Try a different browser

### Lost my progress?
- Check if you saved with the **Save** button
- Progress is stored in your browser
- Don't clear your browser data/cookies
- Use **Export** to back up your character

### Combat is too hard?
- Lower difficulty in Settings (⚙️)
- Rest more often to stay at full HP
- Recruit companions to help
- Use potions and items
- Try defensive tactics in combat

### Combat is too easy?
- Increase difficulty in Settings (⚙️)
- Try playing without companions
- Use aggressive tactics for more challenge
