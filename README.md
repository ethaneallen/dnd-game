## 🎮 How to Play

### **Character Creation**
1. Choose your character name
2. Select a race (Human, Elf, Dwarf, Halfling, Half-Orc, Tiefling, Dragonborn)
3. Pick a class (Fighter, Wizard, Rogue, Cleric, Ranger, Barbarian)
4. Choose a background (Soldier, Scholar, Criminal, Noble, Outlander, Acolyte)
5. Your stats are automatically rolled using D&D 5e rules (4d6 drop lowest)

### **Combat**
- **Attack** - Make weapon or spell attacks
- **Defend** - Increase AC temporarily
- **Use Spell** - Cast prepared spells (spellcasters only)
- **Retreat** - Attempt to flee from battle

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**
- `A` - Attack
- `D` - Defend
- `S` - Cast Spell
- `R` - Retreat

### **Exploration**
- **Explore** - Discover new areas and encounters
- **Travel** - Move to different locations
- **Rest** - Short or long rest to recover HP and spells
- **Party** - Manage your companions
- **Journal** - Review your quest log and discoveries
- **Crafting** - Create items from materials

### **Dice Rolls & Animations**
Every dice roll in the game features immersive 3D animations with 100% coverage:
- **Attack Rolls** - Spinning dice appear in top-right corner when attacking
- **Bonus Attacks** - Additional spinning dice from critical hit bonuses
- **Extra Attacks** - Fighter extra attacks show animated rolls
- **Skill Checks** - All ability checks (perception, investigation, dexterity, etc.) display spinning dice
- **Trap Detection & Disarming** - Both detecting and disarming traps show dice animations
- **Trap Damage** - Even triggering traps displays the damage roll animation
- **Exploration Events** - Town events, gambits, and checks all feature dice rolls
- **Sound Effects** - Unique spinning/wobbling sound effect during each roll

### **🔊 Ambient Soundscapes**
The game includes immersive background audio that dynamically changes based on your location:
- **Toggle On/Off** - Enable or disable ambient soundscapes in Settings
- **Volume Control** - Adjust ambient volume independently from other sounds
- **Location-Aware** - Soundscapes automatically change as you travel:
  - 🔥 **Tavern Fire** - Crackling flames when visiting inns and taverns
  - 🌲 **Forest Wind** - Wind through trees in wilderness and forest areas
  - 🕳️ **Cave Ambience** - Echoing drips in caves and crypts
  - 🏘️ **Town Square** - Bustling activity in towns and settlements
  - 💀 **Dungeon Drips** - Eerie sounds in dungeons and dark places
- **Seamless Loops** - Continuous ambient audio that doesn't interrupt gameplay
- **Persistent Settings** - Your preferences are saved between sessions
- **Visual Feedback** - Golden glow for critical hits (natural 20), dark grey for fumbles (natural 1)
- **Advantage/Disadvantage** - Dual dice animations for advantage and disadvantage rolls

### **Progression**
- Gain XP by defeating enemies and completing quests
- Level up to increase HP, abilities, and unlock new spells
- Find and equip better weapons and armor
- Recruit companions to aid you in battle
- Unlock achievements for special accomplishments
